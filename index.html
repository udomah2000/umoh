profile

Dr. Kofi hails from Akwa ibom state. He attended St Patrick's college primary school, Ikot Ansa, Barracks Road Calabar. He is an alumnus of the University of Uyo, where he obtained his B.Sc and Ph.D (Comparative Politics). He is presently a lecturer in the Department of Political Science and Public Administration, University of uyo, Akwa ibom state. His research interests are in the areas of Comparative Politics, Public Policy, Nigeria Government and Politics, International Politics and Methodology. He has many publications, Local and International to his credit he is also a Fellow of the Institute of Policy Management Development. He is married and blessed with children.
